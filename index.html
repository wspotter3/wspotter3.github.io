About Me

I am currently a senior at Towson University located in Towson, Maryland. I am on course to graduate with a BA in Illustration in the spring of 2015. My style of art is very unique in that it is easily distinguishable from other illustrators. I have a fun creative way of designing characters, landscapes, and ideas. I can work through multiple mediums, with my preference being digital. I am very versatile and have a vast knowledge with adobe products which gives me an edge when doing graphic design or illustration.

The Website I chose is http://johnkavanagh.co.uk/
I chose this website because of the clean look and interactivity it presented. I liked how his header would change every few seconds and it would type a new one once it erased the one before. I also enjoyed the small design details in how the home page and portfolio were formatted.
